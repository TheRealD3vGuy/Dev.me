About the Season

DECODE™ presented by RTX

Starting September 6, 2025, FIRST Tech Challenge teams investigate the power of the world's artifacts and unlock mysteries in an exciting new challenge. Teams program classroom-scale robots to follow autonomous commands before student drivers take control in thrilling two-on-two matches.

On and off the field, students develop STEM skills, engage in community outreach, practice engineering innovation, and build confidence to help them succeed in the future.

Competition Awards

Inspire Award

Presented to the team that truly embodies the challenge of FTC. Strong ambassadors for FIRST who promote the program in their community.

Think Award

Recognizes the team that best reflects the journey of the engineering design process, documented in their engineering portfolio.

Connect Award

Celebrates teams that most connect with their local STEM community and demonstrate effective outreach efforts.
